Ganzfeld's Chamber

Ganzfelds Chamber is a collaborative art project which explores sound and representation through a scope of media, presented in a unique digital space. In its early stages, the work developed like an exquisite corpse; our responses had been driven by the unique and telling sounds produced by Jesse Hitchman as well as the lucid documentation of these sounds through spectrograms produced by Huda Caglayan. These elicited such concepts as the fever dream, resonance, image noise and the artefact. Each artist had to dive deep into their practice - having never considered such circumstances like the COVID-19 pandemic - and pull meaning from the sounds of our private lives.
